2. How do I change my number of dependencies/exemptions?

<!–/html_preserve–>

A W-4 form describes the number of dependencies/exemptions you have. This is important because payroll will tax you differently depending on the number of dependencies. Dependencies are most commonly children, but you may have other exemptions.

First, go to your payroll information as described here. Then

  • Click “Federal and State Withholding”
  • Change Exemptions
